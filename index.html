Cách giải phương trình bậc 4

\(ax^4+bx^3+cx^2+dx+e=0\)

\(\Leftrightarrow x^4+{b\over a}x^3+{c\over a}x^2+{d\over a}x+{e\over a}=0\)

Đặt \(x=y-\frac{b}{4a}\) phương trình trở thành

\(\left(y-{b\over4a}\right)^4+{b\over a}\left(y-{b\over4a}\right)^3+{c\over a}\left(y-{b\over4a}\right)^2+{d\over a}\left(y-{b\over4a}\right)+{e\over a}=0\)

\(\Leftrightarrow y^4 - \frac{b}{a} y^3 + \frac{6b^2}{16a^2} y^2 - \frac{4b^3}{64a^3} y + \frac{b^4}{256a^4} + \frac{b}{a} \left( y^3 - \frac{3b}{4a} y^2 + \frac{3b^2}{16a^2} y - \frac{b^3}{64a^3} \right) + \frac{c}{a} \left( y^2 - \frac{b}{2a} y + \frac{b^2}{16a^2} \right) + \frac{d}{a} \left( y - \frac{b}{4a} \right) + \frac{e}{a} = 0\)

\(\Leftrightarrow y^4 - \frac{b}{a}y^3 + \frac{3b^2}{8a^2}y^2 - \frac{b^3}{16a^3}y + \frac{b^4}{256a^4} + \frac{b}{a}y^3 - \frac{3b^2}{4a^2}y^2 + \frac{3b^3}{16a^3}y - \frac{b^4}{64a^4} + \frac{c}{a}y^2 - \frac{bc}{2a^2}y + \frac{b^2c}{16a^3} + \frac{d}{a}y - \frac{bd}{4a^2} + \frac{e}{a} = 0 \)

\(\Leftrightarrow y^4 + \left(\frac{c}{a}-\frac{3b^2}{8a^2} \right)y^2 + \left( \frac{b^3}{8a^3} - \frac{bc}{2a^2} + \frac{d}{a} \right)y + \frac{b^2c}{16a^3} - \frac{bd}{4a^2} -\frac{3b^4}{256a^4} + \frac{e}{a} = 0 \)

\(\Leftrightarrow y^4+\left(\frac{8ac-3b^2}{8a^2}\right)y^2+\left(\frac{b^3-4abc+8a^2d}{8a^3}\right)y+\frac {16ab^2c-64a^2bd-3b^4+256a^3e}{256a^4}=0 \)

Đặt \( p=\frac{8ac-3b^2}{8a^2},\ q=\frac{b^3-4abc+8a^2d}{8a^3},\ r=\frac {16ab^2c-64a^2bd-3b^4+256a^3e}{256a^4} \) ta được

\(y^4+py^2+qy+r=0\)

\(\Leftrightarrow y^4+py^2=-qy-r \)

\(\Leftrightarrow y^4+2py^2+p^2=py^2+p^2-qy-r \)

\(\Leftrightarrow (y^2+p)^2=py^2+p^2-qy-r \)
