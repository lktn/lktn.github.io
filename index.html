Cách giải phương trình bậc 2

\(\quad\ ax^2+bx+c=0\ (a\neq0)\)

\(\Leftrightarrow ax^2+bx=-c\)

\(\Leftrightarrow x^2+\dfrac{b}ax=-\dfrac{c}a\)

\(\Leftrightarrow x^2+2x\cdot\dfrac{b}{2a}=-\dfrac{c}a\)

\(\Leftrightarrow x^2+2x\cdot\dfrac{b}{2a}+\left(\dfrac{b}{2a}\right)^2=\left(\dfrac{b}{2a}\right)^2-\dfrac{c}a\)

\(\Leftrightarrow \left(x+\dfrac{b}{2a}\right)^2=\dfrac{b^2}{4a^2}-\dfrac{c}a\)

\(\Leftrightarrow \left(x+\dfrac{b}{2a}\right)^2=\dfrac{b^2-4ac}{4a^2}\)

\(\Leftrightarrow x+\dfrac{b}{2a}=\pm\sqrt{\dfrac{b^2-4ac}{4a^2}}\)

\(\Leftrightarrow x+\dfrac{b}{2a}=\pm\dfrac{\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}\)

\(\Leftrightarrow x=-\dfrac{b}{2a}\pm\dfrac{\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}\)

\(\Leftrightarrow x=\dfrac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}\)

Cách giải phương trình bậc 3

\(\quad\ ax^3+bx^2+cx+d=0\ (a\neq0)\)

\(\Leftrightarrow x^3+\dfrac{b}{a}x^2+\dfrac{c}ax+\frac{d}a=0\)

Đặt \(x=y-\dfrac{b}{3a}\) phương trình trở thành:

\(\Leftrightarrow \left(y-\dfrac{b}{3a}\right)^3+\dfrac{b}{a}\left(y-\dfrac{b}{3a}\right)^2+\dfrac{c}a\left(y-\dfrac{b}{3a}\right)+\dfrac{d}a=0\\\)

\(\Leftrightarrow y^3-\dfrac{b}ay^2+\dfrac{b^2}{3a^2}y-\dfrac{b^3}{27a^3}+\dfrac{b}a\left(y^2-\dfrac{2b}{3a}y+\dfrac{b^2}{9a^2}\right)+\dfrac{c}{a}\left(y-\dfrac{b}{3a}\right)+\dfrac{d}a=0\)

\(\Leftrightarrow y^3-\dfrac{b}ay^2+\dfrac{b^2}{3a^2}y-\dfrac{b^3}{27a^3}+\dfrac{b}ay^2-\dfrac{2b^2}{3a^2}y+\dfrac{b^3}{9a^3}+\dfrac{c}{a}y-\dfrac{bc}{3a^2}+\dfrac{d}a=0\)

\(\Leftrightarrow y^3+\left(\dfrac{c}a-\dfrac{b^2}{3a^2}\right)y+\left(\dfrac{2b^3}{27a^3}-\dfrac{bc}{3a^2}+\dfrac{d}a\right)=0\)

Đặt \(p=\dfrac{c}a-\dfrac{b^2}{3a^2},\ q=\dfrac{2b^3}{27a^3}-\dfrac{bc}{3a^2}+\dfrac{d}a\) ta được

\(\quad\ y^3+py+q=0\)

Đặt \(y=u+v\) sao cho \(uv=-\dfrac{p}3\), phương trình trở thành

\(\quad\ (y+v)^3+p(u+v)+q=0 \)

\(\Leftrightarrow u^3+v^3+3u^2v+3uv^2+p(u+v)=-q\)

\(\Leftrightarrow u^3+v^3+3uv(u+v)+p(u+v)=-q\)

\(\Leftrightarrow u^3+v^3+(u+v)(3uv+p)=-q\)

\(\Leftrightarrow u^3+v^3+(u+v)(3\cdot\dfrac{-p}3+p)=-q\)

\(\Leftrightarrow u^3+v^3=-q\)

Ta có: \(\begin{cases}u^3+v^3=-q \\uv=-\dfrac{p}{3}\end{cases}\Leftrightarrow \begin{cases}u^3+v^3=-q \\u^3v^3=-\dfrac{p^3}{27}\end{cases}\)

\(u^3, v^3\) là nghiệm của phương trình: \(X^2+qX-\dfrac{p^3}{27}=0\)

\(u^3=-\dfrac{q}2+\sqrt{\dfrac{q^2}4+\dfrac{p^3}{27}}\Leftrightarrow u=\sqrt[3]{-\dfrac{q}2+\sqrt{\dfrac{q^2}4+\dfrac{p^3}{27}}}\)

\(v^3=-\dfrac{q}2-\sqrt{\dfrac{q^2}4+\dfrac{p^3}{27}}\Leftrightarrow v=\sqrt[3]{-\dfrac{q}2-\sqrt{\dfrac{q^2}4+\dfrac{p^3}{27}}}\)

\(\quad\ y=\sqrt[3]{-\dfrac{q}2+\sqrt{\dfrac{q^2}4+\dfrac{p^3}{27}}}+\sqrt[3]{\dfrac{q}2-\sqrt{\dfrac{q^2}4+\dfrac{p^3}{27}}}\quad\) vì \(y=u+v\)

\(\Leftrightarrow y=\sqrt[3]{-\dfrac{q}2+\sqrt{\left(\dfrac{q}2\right)^2+\left(\dfrac{p}3\right)^3}}+\sqrt[3]{-\dfrac{q}2-\sqrt{\left(\dfrac{q}2\right)^2+\left(\dfrac{p}3\right)^3}}\)

\(\Leftrightarrow y=\sqrt[3]{\dfrac{-b^3}{27a^3}+\dfrac{bc}{6a^2}-\dfrac{d}{2a}+\sqrt{\left(\dfrac{-b^3}{27a^3}+\dfrac{bc}{6a^2}-\dfrac{d}{2a}\right)^2+\left(\dfrac{c}{3a}-\dfrac{b^2}{9a^2}\right)^3}}+\sqrt[3]{\dfrac{-b^3}{27a^3}+\dfrac{bc}{6a^2}-\dfrac{d}{2a}-\sqrt{\left(\dfrac{-b^3}{27a^3}+\dfrac{bc}{6a^2}-\dfrac{d}{2a}\right)^2+\left(\dfrac{c}{3a}-\dfrac{b^2}{9a^2}\right)^3}}\)

\(\Leftrightarrow x=\sqrt[3]{\dfrac{-b^3}{27a^3}+\dfrac{bc}{6a^2}-\dfrac{d}{2a}+\sqrt{\left(\dfrac{-b^3}{27a^3}+\dfrac{bc}{6a^2}-\dfrac{d}{2a}\right)^2+\left(\dfrac{c}{3a}-\dfrac{b^2}{9a^2}\right)^3}}+\sqrt[3]{\dfrac{-b^3}{27a^3}+\dfrac{bc}{6a^2}-\dfrac{d}{2a}-\sqrt{\left(\dfrac{-b^3}{27a^3}+\dfrac{bc}{6a^2}-\dfrac{d}{2a}\right)^2+\left(\dfrac{c}{3a}-\dfrac{b^2}{9a^2}\right)^3}}-\dfrac{b}{3a}\)

Cách giải phương trình bậc 4

\(\quad\ ax^4+bx^3+cx^2+dx+e=0\ (a\neq0)\)

\(\Leftrightarrow x^4+\dfrac{b}{a}x^3+\dfrac{c}ax^2+\dfrac{d}ax+\dfrac{e}a=0\)

Đặt \(x=y-\dfrac{b}{4a}\) phương trình trở thành:

\(\quad\ \left(y-\dfrac{b}{4a}\right)^4+\dfrac{b}a\left(y-\dfrac{b}{4a}\right)^3+\dfrac{c}a\left(y-\dfrac{b}{4a}\right)^2+\dfrac{d}a\left(y-\dfrac{b}{4a}\right)+\dfrac{e}a=0\)

\(\Leftrightarrow y^4-\dfrac{b}{a}y^3+\dfrac{6b^2}{16a^2}y^2-\dfrac{4b^3}{64a^3}y+\dfrac{b^4}{256a^4}+\dfrac{b}{a}\left(y^3-\dfrac{3b}{4a} y^2 + \dfrac{3b^2}{16a^2} y - \dfrac{b^3}{64a^3} \right) + \dfrac{c}{a} \left( y^2 - \dfrac{b}{2a} y + \dfrac{b^2}{16a^2} \right) + \dfrac{d}{a} \left( y - \dfrac{b}{4a} \right) + \dfrac{e}{a} = 0\)

\(\Leftrightarrow y^4-\dfrac{b}{a}y^3+\dfrac{3b^2}{8a^2}y^2-\dfrac{b^3}{16a^3}y+\dfrac{b^4}{256a^4}+\dfrac{b}{a}y^3-\dfrac{3b^2}{4a^2}y^2 + \dfrac{3b^3}{16a^3}y - \dfrac{b^4}{64a^4} + \dfrac{c}{a}y^2 - \dfrac{bc}{2a^2}y + \dfrac{b^2c}{16a^3} + \dfrac{d}ay - \dfrac{bd}{4a^2} + \dfrac{e}{a} = 0 \)

\(\Leftrightarrow y^4+\left(\dfrac{c}a-\dfrac{3b^2}{8a^2}\right)y^2+\left(\dfrac{b^3}{8a^3}-\dfrac{bc}{2a^2}+\dfrac{d}{a} \right)y+\dfrac{b^2c}{16a^3} - \dfrac{bd}{4a^2} -\dfrac{3b^4}{256a^4} + \dfrac{e}{a} = 0 \)

\(\Leftrightarrow y^4+\left(\dfrac{8ac-3b^2}{8a^2}\right)y^2+\left(\dfrac{b^3-4abc+8a^2d}{8a^3}\right)y+\dfrac{16ab^2c-64a^2bd-3b^4+256a^3e}{256a^4}=0 \)

Đặt \( p=\dfrac{8ac-3b^2}{8a^2},\ q=\dfrac{b^3-4abc+8a^2d}{8a^3},\ r=\dfrac {16ab^2c-64a^2bd-3b^4+256a^3e}{256a^4} \) ta được

\(\quad\ y^4+py^2+qy+r=0\)

\(\Leftrightarrow y^4+py^2=-qy-r \)

\(\Leftrightarrow y^4+2py^2+p^2=py^2+p^2-qy-r \)

\(\Leftrightarrow (y^2+p)^2=py^2+p^2-qy-r \)

\(\Leftrightarrow\cdots\)
