\(\displaystyle \frac{\ln\zeta(s)}s=\int_0^\infty\Pi(x)x^{-s-1}dx\)

\(\displaystyle \Pi(x)=\frac1{2\pi i}\int_{a-i\infty}^{a+i\infty}\frac{\ln\zeta(s)}sx^sds \)

Fourier Series

\(\displaystyle f(x)=a_0+\sum_{n=1}^\infty\left(a_n\cos\left(\frac{2\pi n}Tx\right)+b_n\sin\left(\frac{2\pi n}Tx\right)\right)\)

\(\displaystyle a_0=\frac1T\int_0^Tf(x)dx\)

\(\displaystyle a_n=\frac2T\int_0^Tf(x)\cos\left(\frac{2\pi n}Tx\right)dx\)

\(\displaystyle b_n=\frac2T\int_0^Tf(x)\sin\left(\frac{2\pi n}Tx\right)dx\)


\(\displaystyle f(x)=\sum_{n=-\infty}c_ne^{i\frac{2\pi n}Tx}\)

\(\displaystyle c_n=\frac1T\int_0^Tf(x)e^{-i\frac{2\pi n}Tx}dx\)
